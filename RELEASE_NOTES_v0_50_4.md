# Macro Regime Scanner v0.50.4 — Four-Quad Axis Scores

This iteration keeps the simple four-regime Growth / Inflation Regime map and adds the two visible axis scores the regime is based on.

## Product logic

The quad card is an executive summary, not a debug panel. It shows one regime, one axis line, two signed axis scores, and one short read.

## Rules

- Four regimes only: Goldilocks, Reflation, Stagflation, Deflation.
- No blend states.
- No transition language.
- No confidence score.
- No price data.
- Growth Score and Inflation Score are signed factor tallies from the evidence used to classify the regime.
- Main scanner scoring is unchanged.
