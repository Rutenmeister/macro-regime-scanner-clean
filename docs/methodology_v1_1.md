# Edgefield Macro Regime Scanner v1.1 Methodology

## Purpose

Macro Regime Scanner ranks public-source macro/fundamental pressure without using price in the live score. The tool is a research terminal, not a signal service.

## Regime model

The current regime card uses two axes:

- **Growth Score**: live public-source factors that describe growth, demand, labor, credit, liquidity, and financial conditions.
- **Inflation Score**: live public-source factors that describe inflation, rates, policy, energy, agriculture, food, weather, and supply pressure.

The primary strict read maps the signs of those two scores into four regimes:

- Growth positive, inflation negative: Goldilocks.
- Growth positive, inflation positive: Reflation / inflationary growth.
- Growth negative, inflation positive: Stagflationary pressure.
- Growth negative, inflation negative: Deflationary pressure.

## Sensitivity read

The sensitivity read is not a second headline regime. It is a controlled note showing where the broader interpretation could land if the contested axis is weighted differently. In the current inflation-positive environment, the main debate is usually whether growth resilience deserves enough weight to call the backdrop reflationary, or whether growth drag is large enough to call it stagflationary pressure.

## Score interpretation

Raw scores are uncapped evidence-pressure tallies. Larger absolute values mean more weighted evidence inside this scanner. They do not mean probabilities and they do not guarantee price movement.

## Asset evidence

Each asset row separates counted rows, context rows, excluded rows, source freshness, top drivers, conflicts, and caveats. Missing, stale, candidate, display-only, or not-live rows should not silently become neutral.

## Known limits

- Mostly U.S.-centered public-source inputs.
- No price data in the live score.
- Source data can be delayed, revised, stale, missing, or incorrect.
- Macro pressure does not equal trade timing.
- Historical validation should be used to test whether pressure readings later align with asset behavior.
