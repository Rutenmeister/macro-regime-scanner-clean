Inputs / filters

Universe Controls

No market values are entered here. This edition filters official/public-source fundamental evidence across macro, policy, positioning, energy, agriculture, credit, liquidity, and rates.

Evidence rows
Default view hides rows that are not live-scored or live-context for the selected asset. Toggle on for full audit context.
Methodology note: This edition displays uncapped raw net pressure scores. Missing data is not treated as neutral. Candidate, sample, stale, display-only, and not-live rows are visible for audit context but do not silently drive the headline score.
Trust guide
  1. Not a buy/sell signal.
  2. Scores rank public-source pressure evidence.
  3. Open any row to see what counted and what was excluded.
  4. Focus on raw pressure, alignment, conflict, freshness, and change.
