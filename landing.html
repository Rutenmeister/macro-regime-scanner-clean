What it does

Compresses official/public-source macro lanes into raw pressure readings across U.S.-centered assets.

What it shows

Raw pressure, confidence, freshness, conflict, release-calendar risk, source health, and asset score audits.

What it is not

Not a price predictor, trade signal, financial adviser, or performance-guarantee system.

New in v0.50: Growth / Inflation Pressure Map

The scanner now summarizes current public-source evidence into a simple macro quad: growth pressure versus inflation/policy pressure. It shows four main states, four blend states, and one low-conviction state. It does not use price and does not replace individual asset score audits.
