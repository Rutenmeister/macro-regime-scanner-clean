# Macro Regime Scanner v0.50.3 — Simple Four-Quad Regime Display Map

v0.50.3 replaces the overly cautious quad snapshot with a simpler four-regime map.

## What changed

- Removed the earlier over-complicated regime logic.
- Removed quad confidence output.
- Removed low-conviction/neutral regime output.
- Kept the quad map price-free.
- Growth and inflation axes now resolve to positive or negative only.
- Current regime can only be Goldilocks, Reflation, Stagflation, or Deflation.
- The app panel was simplified to show the regime, axis signs, scores, and drivers.

## Regime definitions

- Goldilocks: Growth positive / Inflation negative.
- Reflation: Growth positive / Inflation positive.
- Stagflation: Growth negative / Inflation positive.
- Deflation: Growth negative / Inflation negative.

## Integrity notes

This is a summary lens, not a trading signal. It does not change the asset-level scoring engine and does not add price data.
